Biography

Xiangru received his PhD degree in Environmental Engineering from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He is Director of the Environmental Engineering and Management Program and a Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.
His research interests include innovative water and wastewater treatment processes, and emerging micropollutant detection, toxicity and control. He has generated 100 journal papers, 60 of which are published in Nature Water, Nature Communications, Science Bulletin, Environmental Science & Technology and Water Research.
In recognition of his achievements in the field of disinfection byproducts, he was invited to give plenary presentations at the “Gordon Research Conference on Drinking Water Disinfection Byproducts” four consecutive times in 2012, 2015, 2017 and 2019. He is selected to serve as Associate Editor of two Elsevier journals, Chemosphere and Trends in Environmental Analytical Chemistry.
